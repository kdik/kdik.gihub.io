I have made video games in the past

You have reached the personal web page of the visionary computer game maker Karolis Dikcius, best known for games such as Desert of Vice & Ladderhead.. I am based in Vilnius, Lithuania and I have been doing whatever I have been doing since 1995.

When I am not making games I enjoy reading literature, watching films and taking my dog on a walk. In the past I have also enjoyed making analog electric guitar effect circuits.

My most recent project is Ladderhead, a text adventure about a man bound to a time and a place in the aether.
