Strange Aeons

Strange Aeons is a microphone-controlled game set in a cyber-hell and made with a gnostic mindset.

You are stuck in a gnostic cyber-hell. The followers of your cult are summoning you. Chat with a neural net AI through the microphone to escape hell and return to the land of the living. Engage in a strange ritual and figure out why you were banished and cast out to hell in the first place.

This is a neural-net-AI, speech-to-text and text-to-speech powered experience. All of this works offline, without a back-end server or internet connectivity.

It's all very, very cool (& it's powered by the Godot engine).

Features:

  • Microphone & Keyboard controls
  • Neural Net AI
  • Procedural music
  • Imaginary languages by @cesque
  • Optional VR mode
