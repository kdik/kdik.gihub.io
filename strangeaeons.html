Strange Aeons

Strange Aeons is a game where you escape a cyber-hell as you chat with a neural net AI. The only way to interact is through your microphone.

It's all very, very cool.

Current features:

  • Chatting with an AI through the microphone.
  • Inferno, by Dante Alighieri.
  • A 10 minute demo.

Planned features:

  • Procedural language generation by @cesque.
  • Out of the box Linux support.
  • MacOS support.
  • VR support.
  • Making the game even more high concept.
