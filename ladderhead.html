Ladderhead

“‘Ladderhead’ is a game about a man in trouble.” - Karolis Dikcius

My next game, Ladderhead, will tell the story about a man bound to a time and a place in the aether. Seeking your loved one you will traverse a curious building, each floor being a perilous mystery. Every time you die the day resets.

Ladderhead is a work in progress. It will come out some time this year.
