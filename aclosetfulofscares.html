A Closetful of Scares

A young man's move into a new apartment becomes a hellish ordeal when he finds out it is inhabited by a haunted wardrobe.

A Closetful of Scares is an upcoming short film co-written/co-directed by Mantas Narkevičius and Karolis Dikčius. It was shot in 4:3 in color and in B&W in Autumn of 2020 and is expected to be released around Halloween 2021.
